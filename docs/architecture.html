▼
AIBMR
Brand research. Scrapes the brand site + socials → company profile: description, ICP, geo, tone-of-voice, decision-makers, niche classification.
AI-MM
AI Matchmaker. Scores the creator network against a brand profile (niche · audience · geo alignment) → ranked shortlist of creators.
AICREN
Creator enrichment. For a creator record: refreshes follower count, engagement, email, per-platform handles, language and last-post recency from the platforms.
AICROPS
Creator outreach. Drafts the personalised first-touch message per creator (in the brand's tone, matched to the creator's niche) and schedules the multi-step follow-up sequence, with send-times tuned to historical response curves.
Twenty CRM
System of record. Stores Companies, Persons & Opportunities and the creator network (the source of the matching pool).
